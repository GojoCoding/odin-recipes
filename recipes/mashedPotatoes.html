Mashed Potatoes

Recipe provided by Delish

Ingredients

  • 3 lb. mixed potatoes, such as russets & Yukon Golds
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/2 c. (1 stick) unsalted butter, plus 2 tablespoons for garnish
  • 1/2 c. whole milk
  • 1/2 c. sour cream
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Once you've tried these spuds, you'll never need another mashed potatoes recipe

  • Yields
  • Prep Time
  • Total Time
  • 6 - 8 serving(s)
  • 25 mins
  • 30 mins

Step 1

Spread 1 tablespoon butter on one side of each slice of bread. With butter side down, top each slice of bread with about Â½ cup cheddar.

Step 2

In a skillet over medium heat, melt 1 tablespoon butter. Add two slices of bread, butter side down. Cook until bread is golden and cheese is starting to melt, about 2 minutes. Flip one piece of bread on top of the other and continue to cook until cheese is melty, about 30 seconds more.

Step 3

Repeat for the second sandwich, wiping skillet clean if necessary.

Step 4

Pour over warm milk-butter mixture and stir until completely combined and creamy. Add sour cream and stir until combined.

Step 5

Season mashed potatoes generously with salt and pepper.

Step 6

Transfer potatoes to a serving bowl and top with remaining two tablespoons butter. Season with more pepper before serving.
